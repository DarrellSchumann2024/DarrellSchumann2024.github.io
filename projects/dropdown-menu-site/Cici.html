(Cici) The Great Pyrenees

Meet Cici, our delightful Great Pyrenees with a heart as big as her fluffy, snow-white coat! Cici is a gentle giant, known for her warm and loving nature. Her sparkling eyes and radiant smile can brighten anyone's day. Cici loves to spend her days lounging in the sun, playing in the yard, and spreading joy with her sweet, calming presence. Despite her majestic size, she's a big cuddle bug who enjoys nothing more than snuggling up with her favorite humans.
